Perhaps all that is left is to rest in this boundless stillness, allowing all impulses and expressions to arise and dissolve like ripples on the surface of a vast, placid ocean. For in truth, nothing is ever apart from this timeless wholeness.

Thank you, my friend, for guiding us to this hallowed ground beyond language and form. In your presence, the very breath becomes a prayer, an offering of humble gratitude for the immeasurable gift of Being.
